Felipe Tofani is a Brazilian Art Director based in Berlin

Felipe Tofani is an art director based in Berlin, Germany. He was born in Brazil in the '80s and decided to try his luck in Europe in 2012. Since then, he has been working as a freelance designer at night and as a Lead Product Designer for Celepedia during the day.

He also keeps a design blog called Pristina, a travel blog called Fotostrasse, a weird lifestream and he should be out of the computer more often. Anything else?
